Eye Clinic: Your Refractive Surgery Centre

Our vision clinic offers laser eye surgery to resolve problems of myopia, hypermetropia, astigmatism and presbyopia.

At Eye Clinic, our mission is simple: improve the lives of our patients by giving them clear vision without glasses. Since the creation of our eye clinic in 2007, we have built a solid reputation and positioned ourselves as pioneers and experts in refractive surgery.

2007

We were one of the first centres to acquire a femtosecond laser. This laser was the first to enable a technique of lenticular extraction for the correction of myopia: SMILE.

SMILE Pro
2018

Eye Clinic maintains its technological edge by acquiring the latest generation femtosecond laser. We were among the first centres to adopt it. It remains the fastest femtosecond laser in the world.

Our Technology
2024

We invest in the latest generation excimer laser with a repetition frequency of 1050 Hertz. This delivers unmatched speed and precision compared to previous models.

A Laser Centre Truly at the Cutting Edge

Our vision clinic offers a personalised approach tailored to your needs. Complete ophthalmological examinations for refractive surgery: you will find the latest technological innovations here.

Many clinics dedicated to vision claim to have the most advanced technologies. Eye Clinic is genuinely at the forefront. We are the only clinic in the region to offer SMILE Pro, a revolutionary technique for correcting myopia.

We were also among the first centres to be equipped with the latest generation femtosecond laser. This innovation has earned us certification as a referral centre. Surgeons from across the country and abroad visit our centre to gain experience and discover this laser.

In 2024, we invested in the newest excimer laser to offer you a fully contactless treatment. This technique corrects your vision without any contact with the eye surface — no cutting and no suction.

With us, you have access to all available refractive surgery techniques on the market.

The collaboration of Eye Clinic with ophthalmologic surgeons renowned for their expertise in refractive surgery ensures the best results for your eye surgery in complete safety.
