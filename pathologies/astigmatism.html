What Is Astigmatism?

Astigmatism is an extremely common visual defect affecting approximately one in three people. It causes blurred or distorted vision at all distances — both near and far.

The condition is caused by an irregularly shaped cornea. Instead of being round like a football, the astigmatic cornea is shaped more like a rugby ball. This irregular curvature prevents light from focusing evenly on the retina, creating two different focal points.

Astigmatism is often present from birth and frequently occurs alongside myopia or hypermetropia. Symptoms include blurred vision, eye strain, headaches, and difficulty with night driving.

Professional management through specialized corrective lenses is the most common and effective way to restore clear vision for those with astigmatism.

Managing Astigmatism Professionally

At Eye Clinic, we specialize in the custom management of astigmatism through advanced clinical assessments and precision optics.

We offer multiple solutions depending on your lifestyle and the level of correction required:

  • Specialist Toric Lenses — Precision contact lenses designed to stay stable on the eye for crystal clear vision.
  • Bespoke Spectacle Lenses — Custom-ground lenses that compensate perfectly for your unique corneal shape.
  • Digital Mapping — Using high-resolution topography to understand the exact shape and curvature of your eye.

Astigmatism management is integrated into our comprehensive eye examinations, ensuring your ocular health is monitored as well as your vision.
