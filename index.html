How to Get Your Eyes Treated?

A pre-operative assessment before any intervention

By contacting Eye Clinic, we will provide you with all the information you need for your laser eye surgery. Following a complete ophthalmological examination with one of our surgeons, they will specify a diagnosis and the surgical technique to consider.

The procedure is carried out under local anaesthesia (eye drops) and lasts approximately 20 minutes for a life free from glasses!

What Sets Us Apart

A Truly Cutting-Edge Laser Centre

Eye Clinic is equipped with the latest generation femtosecond laser platform. Faster and more precise, it performs a cut in just 4 to 10 seconds depending on the technique.

Our specialist clinic is at the forefront of vision technology. As such, Eye Clinic has been chosen as a referral centre for refractive surgery.

The Only Clinic Offering SMILE Pro

In addition to LASIK and PRK procedures, we perform SMILE Pro — the latest generation of minimally invasive refractive surgery. Eye Clinic is the only refractive surgery centre to offer SMILE Pro in the region.

This surgical technique is exclusively available on the latest platform. It enables faster recovery and reduces risks associated with flap creation as in standard LASIK.
