Your Vision Care Journey in 3 Steps

Initial consultation, comprehensive examination, and ongoing follow-up: find all the practical information about your care at Eye Clinic.

1

Initial Consultation & Discovery

Understanding Your Needs

Your journey begins with a conversation. We take the time to understand your lifestyle, visual requirements, and any concerns you might have. This initial session is about setting the foundation for your personalised care plan.

This consultation lasts approximately 30-45 minutes, during which our expert optometrists will discuss your history and perform preliminary screening. We use advanced technology to get an initial profile of your vision health.

Practical Information

  • Allow 45 minutes of your time
  • Bring your current glasses or contact lens details
  • Have a list of any medications you are currently taking
  • Think about your typical visual environment (screens, driving, sports)
2

The Clinical Examination

We use the most advanced diagnostic tools to ensure the highest standard of care.

1. Digital Mapping

We use high-resolution digital imaging to create a detailed map of your eyes. This is non-invasive and provides a benchmark for your future eye health monitoring.

2. Precise Refraction

Our optometrists perform a highly accurate measurement of your prescription, ensuring your vision correction is perfectly tailored to your unique requirements.

3. Health Screening

We perform internal screenings for glaucoma, cataracts, and retinal health. Our digital technology allows us to see details that traditional methods might miss.

4. Personalised Plan

Following the examination, we discuss the results with you in detail and recommend the best vision correction or management plan for your needs.

3

Ongoing Support & Care

At Eye Clinic, our relationship doesn't end with a single visit. We are committed to your long-term vision health and provide ongoing support for all our patients.

Whether it's adjusting a new pair of glasses, fitting your first contact lenses, or managing a chronic condition like dry eye, we are here to help whenever you need us.

There are several ways we support you: we send regular reminders for your health check-ups, provide emergency advice for any sudden vision changes, and offer professional adjustments for your eyewear to ensure maximum comfort.

In the majority of cases, regular check-ups are recommended every 12 to 24 months. However, if you are wearing contact lenses or have specific eye health needs, we may recommend more frequent monitoring.
