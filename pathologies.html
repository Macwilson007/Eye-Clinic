Your Pathology

Discover the eye conditions we treat and learn about the laser surgery solutions available at Eye Clinic.

Conditions We Treat

Myopia

Difficulty seeing objects at a distance. Myopia occurs when the eye is too long, causing light to focus in front of the retina instead of on it.

Learn more

Astigmatism

Blurred or distorted vision at all distances. Astigmatism is caused by an irregularly shaped cornea that prevents light from focusing properly.

Learn more

Hypermetropia

Difficulty seeing objects up close. The eye is too short, causing light to focus behind the retina. This condition often worsens with age.

Learn more

Presbyopia

Age-related loss of near vision. After 40, the lens gradually loses its ability to focus on close objects, making reading difficult.

Learn more

Which Treatment Is Right for You?

Each eye condition requires a specific surgical approach. During your pre-operative assessment at Eye Clinic, our surgeons will carry out a comprehensive evaluation to determine which technique is best suited to your individual case.

Factors such as corneal thickness, pupil size, prescription strength, and lifestyle all play a role in choosing the ideal procedure. Our team will guide you through every step to ensure the best possible outcome.
