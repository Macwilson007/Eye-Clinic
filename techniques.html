Our Professional Services

Eye Clinic provides the most advanced diagnostic and corrective optometric services. Discover how we can help you see better.

CORE SERVICE

Comprehensive Eye Examinations

Regular Vision & Health Screenings — The Foundation of Good Sight

A standard eye test at Eye Clinic is much more than just a prescription check. We perform a complete health screening using the latest diagnostic technology.

What's Included

We use high-resolution digital imaging to monitor your eye health in incredible detail, allowing for early detection of conditions before they affect your vision.

  • Refraction & Binocular Vision Testing
  • Retinal Photography & OCT Scanning
  • Glaucoma & Cataract Screening
  • Visual Field Analysis
  • Personalised Vision Consultation

Benefits

Early detection, precise prescriptions, and long-term vision health monitoring.

SPECIALIST CARE

Contact Lens Consultations

Expert Fittings for All Prescriptions — Comfort Meets Clarity

Whether you are new to contact lenses or have been told you are "hard to fit", our specialists can find the perfect lens for your eyes.

Our Expertise

We provide access to the widest range of contact lens technologies, including custom-made lenses for complex prescriptions.

  • Daily, Monthly & Extended Wear Lenses
  • Toric Lenses for Astigmatism
  • Multifocal Lenses for Presbyopia
  • Myopia Management Lenses for Children
  • Scleral & RGP Lenses for Complex Corneas

Benefits

Freedom from glasses, superior comfort, and clear vision for all activities.

Dry Eye Management

Specialised Treatment for Ocular Surface Disease

Chronic dry eye is a common condition that can significantly affect your quality of life. At Eye Clinic, we offer advanced clinical management to provide lasting relief.

Our Approach

We use specialized diagnostic mapping to determine the cause of your dry eye and create a bespoke treatment plan tailored to your ocular surface health.

  • Tear Film Analysis & Meibomian Gland Imaging
  • In-clinic Blepharitis Cleaning (Blephex)
  • Heat Treatments & Moisture Management Plans
  • Custom Lubrication & Punctal Plugs

Note: Most dry eye conditions can be significantly improved with the right clinical intervention and consistent home care.
